Welcome to Pirate Time.

 

Summary

Pirates love treasure. How far can you go? Pirate Time is a casual rogue-like pirate game where the goal is to get as many treasures before you succumb to Davy Jone’s Locker.

Information

Platforms: Windows / Mac
Genre: Top down, rogue-like, casual adventure game
Rated: for all

Game Description

It’s time to set sail. You must command a pirate crew through the grand seas. You and your crew have craving for some gold. You decide to venture into the oceans, to look for the loot. It will be a dangerous adventure, filled with monsters, pirates, treasures and more. Be careful, be bold, and try not to die…

Mind Set

Pirate Time is meant to play casually. Losing is a part of the game. The game audience is anyone who enjoys a challenge or casual playing. The game has easy mouse and keyboard inputs that have been simplified as much as possible. The target audience age can range from children to adults of any gender. The game graphics are simple, cartoony and the physical size of the game is modest to run on as many systems with low memory/process power.

Gameplay

Pirate Time is meant to play casually. A typical game can last from a few minutes upwards to twenty minutes. The difficulty of the game increases with each new level, making it more difficult to deal with the battles with enemy pirate ships. Click anywhere on the screen to travel to that location. By pressing the appropriate button on the top-left, you may start a quest or leave the area.

In case you get into a fight with an enemy, you ultimately want to leave combat as soon as possible. You get periodically damage until you can hit a bulls-eye between the two sliders on the battle bar.

You require ale to travel. You require a gunpowder to shoot. You require integrity to stay afloat. Run out and it is game over.

Try to get the highest score of treasure you can before it’s game over.

Input Controls

Keyboard and mouse
Left click: Move pirate ship to location, activate quest or lighthouse
spacebar: Shoot cannon during battle

How to Play

You command a pirate ship with crew. Your goal is to get as many treasures as you can before you die. Death comes if you lose all of your ships integrity (health points), you have no more crew members, or run out of ale (fuel) for traveling. You must travel to islands to begin a quest event. Make the best decision you can, and hopefully come out on top. Your stats and other modifications change how a quest event will unfold.

 Avoid the enemy pirates! They are after the same treasures you are. If they get close to you, a battle will begin and you must destroy them. This can hurt so avoid doing so. Once you have your fill of quests in an area, travel to the lighthouse to enter the next level. As you progress to higher levels, enemy ships will become ever stronger.

Game Flow

  1. Press the play button to begin the game.
  2. Click on an area to move the ship.
  3. Once near an island, click on Quest button to begin the event.
  4. Read the story and click on a decision.
  5. Read the results, and click away.
  6. During a battle, press spacebar to sink the enemy ship.
  7. When near a lighthouse, click Next to enter the next level.
  8. Go through as many islands to gain treasures and get a high score.

    Ship Stats: Resources

    Resources are found on the bottom left display. They are crucial to keeping afloat.

    Ship Integrity
    Your ship’s health. If it reaches zero, game is over.

    Crew Members
    Your mateys. Again, if this reaches zero, game over.

    Ale
    This is your ship’s fuel. If this reaches zero, game over.

    Gun Powder
    You need gunpowder to shoot cannons. Without any gunpowder, you are a sitting duck during batltles.

    Treasure
    The prize! Every pirate’s ultimate goal. This is your score. Try to get a high number!

    Ship Stats: Attributes

    Attributes boost your ship. They do an assortment of things and generally help with quests, traveling and battles.

    Morale
    Higher morale will reduce ale cost for traveling.

    Evil
    Higher evil increases the distance of battle bar stoppers.

    Charisma
    Charisma gives you a bonus of ship integrity at the start of a level

    Notoriety
    Slows down the battle slider during battle.

    Wit
    Wit will give you a bonus of gun powder at the start of a level

    Enemies

    Enemy Pirate ships roam the seas. They are on a constant look for loot and others to attack. Your best bet is to avoid them at all costs. Battling enemies will only cause damage and loss of resources. A good pirate would keep their distance and only fight when necessary. Press spacebar while your bar is between the two sliders (on top). Pressing spacebar at the right time will cause the enemy ship to explode!

    Island Quests

    Your ultimate goal of treasure is reached by going to islands and starting quests. They will give you a choice to make, and they can either be advantageous to the player or harmful. Depending on your ship stats, the quests success rate differs. You must think about the choice to make, depending on your ship stats, and hope for the best. Once all or most island quests are depleted, it is time to move to the lighthouse and enter the next level.

     

    LightHouse

    Reach the lighthouse to get to the next level. It might be a good idea to only go to the next level once you have checked out most or all islands on the current level. The areas will get harder with each level increment, so best of luck and get as many treasures as you can!
