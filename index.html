Welcome to Pirate Time game.

 

Pitch

How far can you go? Pirate Time is a rogue-like pirate game where the goal is to get as many treasures before you succumb to Davy Jone’s Locker.

Information

Platforms: Windows / Mac
Genre: Top down, rogue-like, adventure game
Rated: for all

Input Controls

Keyboard and mouse
Left click: Move pirate ship to location, activate quest or lighthouse
spacebar: Shoot cannon during battle

Game Description

It’s time to set sail. You must command a pirate crew through the grand seas. You and your crew have craving for some gold. You decide to venture into the oceans, to look for the loot. How to Play

You command a pirate ship with crew. Your goal is to get as many treasures as you can before you die. Death comes if you lose all of your ships integrity (health points), you have no more crew members, or run out of ale (fuel) for traveling. You must travel to islands to begin a quest event. Make the best decision you can, and hopefully come out on top. Avoid the enemy pirates! They are after the same treasures you are. If they get close to you, a battle will begin and you must destroy them. This can hurt so avoid doing so. Once you have your fill of quests in an area, travel to the lighthouse to enter the next level. As you progress to higher levels, enemy ships will become ever stronger.

Ship Stats: Resources

Resources are found on the bottom left display. They are crucial to keeping afloat.

Ship Integrity
Your ship’s health. If it reaches zero, game is over.

Crew Members
Your mateys. Again, if this reaches zero, game over.

Ale
This is your ship’s fuel. If this reaches zero, game over.

Gun Powder
You need gunpowder to shoot cannons. Without any gunpowder, you are a sitting duck during batltles.

Treasure
The prize! Every pirate’s ultimate goal. This is your score. Try to get a high number!

Ship Stats: Attributes

Attributes boost your ship. They do an assortment of things and generally help with quests, traveling and battles.

Morale
Higher morale will reduce ale cost for traveling.

Evil
Higher evil increases the distance of battle bar stoppers.

Charisma
Charisma gives you a bonus of ship integrity at the start of a level

Notoriety
Slows down the battle slider during battle.

Wit
Wit will give you a bonus of gun powder at the start of a level

Enemies

Enemy Pirate ships roam the seas. They are on a constant look for loot and others to attack. Your best bet is to avoid them at all costs. Battling enemies will only cause damage and loss of resources. A good pirate would keep their distance and only fight when necessary. Press spacebar while your bar is between the two sliders (on top). Pressing spacebar at the right time will cause the enemy ship to explode!

Island Quests

Your ultimate goal of treasure is reached by going to islands and starting quests. They will give you a choice to make, and they can either be advantageous to the player or harmful. Depending on your ship stats, the quests success rate differs. You must think about the choice to make, depending on your ship stats, and hope for the best. Once all or most island quests are depleted, it is time to move to the lighthouse and enter the next level.

 

LightHouse

Reach the lighthouse to get to the next level. It might be a good idea to only go to the next level once you have checked out most or all islands on the current level. The areas will get harder with each level increment, so best of luck and get as many treasures as you can!
